How to Use the QSPI Coin App

Instructions on how to use the main features of the QSPI Coin app.
Easily understood with YouTube videos.

How to Find Top-Performing Coins Over a Given Period

Quickly spot coins that surged in a short period, check hourly rise rates and volatility, and use advanced settings to explore market trends according to your own criteria.

How to Find Worst-Performing Coins Over a Given Period

Quickly check coins that dropped in a short period, monitor hourly fall rates and volatility, and use advanced settings to explore and sort according to your own criteria.

How to Check Cumulative Trading Volume by Period

View top trading volume coins, examine cumulative trading volume hourly, and use advanced settings to explore and sort according to your preferred time periods.

How to Spot Coins with Surging Volume During a Period

Check coins with sudden volume spikes hourly, explore using your own period settings, and confirm results in order of recent volume increase using the check icon.

How to Find Consecutive Gainers Over a Given Period

Check consecutive rising coins by day, hour, or minute. Adjust detailed units in advanced settings and confirm results in order of minute-by-minute consecutive gains using the check icon.

How to View Caution-Listed Coins at a Glance

Check coins and related info in the Watchlist tab and apply detailed settings in the Explore tab under 'More'.

How to Check the Global Price Difference by Coin

Compare global prices and differences, apply detailed settings in the Explore tab under 'More', and sort coins in ascending or descending order according to price differences.

How to Find Rebounding Coins After a Drop

Select your desired time period in the timelapse section, check percentage changes, set detailed conditions in the Explore tab, and freely sort items using advanced period and condition settings.

How to Get Alerts for Surging Coins Over a Given Period

Tap the top-right alert icon to select rise/fall alerts, turn notifications on, set the desired increase rate, period, and exchange, and receive automatic alerts even when the app is closed.

How to Apply Saved Strategies with One Click

Press the top preset button in the Explore tab to instantly apply saved strategy settings, view rising coins, trends over 30/7/1 days, consecutive rises, moving averages, and overbought status, and freely adjust all settings using the edit button.
