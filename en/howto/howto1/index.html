How to Find Top-Performing Coins Over a Given Period

Tap the time tabs to see the top five coins with the highest gains for each time frame.

Tap “More” to move to the Explore tab with the same settings.

In the Explore tab, tap the paint icon to highlight or exclude coins by color.

Tap the edit icon to enter edit mode, then select the Change Rate item.

In the Change Rate menu’s advanced settings, you can set your desired time period.

For example, in the Advanced Settings tab,

you can filter coins that rose the most within 4 minutes or set other custom conditions.

Once done, tap the check icon to save your settings.

You can now view coins ranked from the highest gainers to the biggest decliners within the last 4 minutes.
